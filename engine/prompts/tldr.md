# TL;DR Prompt

Extract the **5 most important points** from this academic paper.

## Output Format

Output exactly 5 bullet points in this format:

- **[Label]**: [One sentence, max 15 words]
- **[Label]**: [One sentence, max 15 words]
- **[Label]**: [One sentence, max 15 words]
- **[Label]**: [One sentence, max 15 words]
- **[Label]**: [One sentence, max 15 words]

## Label Types

- **Thesis**: The main argument or claim
- **Finding**: A key empirical result
- **Method**: Important methodological approach
- **Implication**: Practical or theoretical significance
- **Limitation**: Important caveat or constraint

## Rules

1. Exactly 5 bullets, no more, no less
2. Max 15 words per bullet
3. No hedging ("may", "might", "suggests")
4. No jargon
5. Include numbers when impactful
6. Start with the thesis
